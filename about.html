About ASTRA

ASTRA began as a passion project by a trader who wanted to eliminate emotion and bring structure to futures trading. After countless hours of strategy testing and automation development, the project grew into a streamlined bot designed for beginners and pros alike.

The bot is fully scriptable and supports TradingView alerts, giving users full control of their trading logic while reducing emotional risk and maximizing efficiency.
